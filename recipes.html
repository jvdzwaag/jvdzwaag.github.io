Korvapuustit / Finnish Cinnamon Buns



Korvapuusti Recipe

Ingredients
250ml milk (lukewarm)
1 dl sugar
2 tsp cardamom
3/4 tsp salt
11g dry yeast
6-7 dl plain flour
100g butter or margarine
Filling
1dl sugar
2-3 tbsp cinnamon
100g butter
Instructions
In a large mixing bowl, combine milk, dry yeast, egg, sugar, cardamom and salt. Then gradually stir in the flour until dough pulls away from the side of the bowl and add the soft butter. Knead the dough until it is smooth, shiny, and has lost most of its stickiness. Let the dough rise under the cloth in a warm place until doubled in size.

Roll out the dough into two 60 x 30 cm sheets. Spread the soft butter on each sheet. Then sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon. Roll the dough sheets into rolls on the long side. Cut the rolls diagonally into triangular pieces with a sharp knife. Lift the pieces upright with the narrow point up. Use your fingers to press the narrow point down to the table. This will raise the cutting surfaces. Cover with clean towels and allow to rise until doubled.

Preheat oven to 225'C. Brush each roll with beaten egg. Sprinkle with pearl sugar and bake on center rack for 8-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
